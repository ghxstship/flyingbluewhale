## Project Overview

**Artist Storyline.** _Marchita_ remains one of the most quietly devastating Latin American records of the decade — heartbreak written by a woman with the discipline to not embellish. The Casa Miami listening party is built around that discipline. No band. No production gloss. The chairs are close, the lights are low, the songs are short, and her voice does the work the room was built to receive.

The format mirrors her preferred performance language: track-by-track walkthrough, a few minutes of context before each, guitar in lap, occasional Spanish-English code-switch. The 30 industry seats sit at the back rail so the front 50 are for the people who bought _Marchita_ on first release.

## Emergency Procedures — Silvana Overlays

Inherits Tier 1 baseline. Silvana-specific add-ons:

- **Voice loss protocol.** Her voice is the entire show. (1) Hot tea + 45-min postpone allowed (Patricia holds via humor — Spotify-supplied story bank). (2) If cancellation forced, audience compensated with signed LP + private Spotify Sessions invite. (3) Capture session privately rescheduled within 30 days.
- **No flash photography.** Contract bans flash. Door signage ES + EN. Photographer uses available light only.
- **No phones up.** Patricia invokes at intro. Door staff have a printed laminate to gently lower phones during songs. Vibe contract, not punitive enforcement.
- **Press/industry lockdown.** 30 industry seats by Spotify-curated list only. No walk-on press adds at door without Spotify approval.
- **Allergy: no flowers in green room.** Floral confined to stage + lobby only.
