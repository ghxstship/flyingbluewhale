## C. Emergency Procedures (late-night overlays on house baseline)

**Medical / Intoxication** — Late-night + full bar raises intoxication risk. 2 BLS medics, dedicated quiet recovery zone in Podcast Studio annex. Bar enforces last-call + water station; refusal-of-service protocol briefed. ODOM security trained in non-escalation removal.

**Fire / Smoke / FX** — CO2 + low-fog are house-approved; haze detector isolation coordinated with Fire Marshal at walkthrough (false-alarm prevention). Three egress paths maintained; dancefloor density monitored against 200 cap. Producer is incident commander.

**Active Threat** — Run/Hide/Fight; sworn officers with MPD radio; lockdown via key-locked green/podcast/IT rooms; DJ has cut-music + house-lights-up emergency cue carded at booth.

**Crowd / Capacity** — Hard 200 count via scan-in/scan-out clicker reconciliation; one-in-one-out at cap. Dancefloor surge monitored by floor manager + security; CO2 cues paused if density spikes.

**Severe Weather** — Patio bar closes on lightning; NHC/NWS monitored T-72; postpone trigger T-48 hurricane watch.

**Power Loss** — UPS holds emergency lighting + comms ≥30 min; DJ rig on protected circuit; house music failover playlist on battery laptop.

**Noise / Neighbor (MiMo residential)** — SPL monitored at property line; late-night permit conditions enforced; patio music capped after 01:00; community-relations contact on call.

**Comms** — Motorola CP200d: ch1 production, ch2 security, ch3 medical, ch4 bar/ops. Backup WhatsApp `T2-{{SLUG}}-Showday`.

**Chain of Command** — Producer → Tigre EP → GHXSTSHIP Dir of Production → Spotify LATAM Casa lead. Halt authority: Safety Director (safety), Producer (operational), Spotify lead (editorial).
