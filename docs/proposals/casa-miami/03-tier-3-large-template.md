**Fire / FX** — CO2/low-fog/confetti house-approved; haze/confetti coordinated with Fire Marshal at walkthrough; confetti cleanup factored into strike (slip hazard). Stage power + pyro-free. Three egress maintained; 300 well under 605 CO.

**Active Threat** — Run/Hide/Fight; 14 security incl. sworn officers w/ MPD radio; show-stop "house up / music cut" cue carded at FOH + every department head; lockdown via key-locked rooms.

**Crowd / Changeover surge** — Highest risk at act changeovers (floor compression toward stage). Floor managers + barricade at stage lip; security front-of-stage detail; changeover DJ bridge keeps energy without a dead-air crush.

**Severe Weather** — Patio + OOH wind-rated; lightning closes patio; NHC/NWS T-72; postpone trigger T-48 hurricane watch; multi-truck load-out contingency for storm.

**Power Loss** — Generator backup on concert rig (Production line); UPS for emergency lighting/comms; show-stop protocol if primary distro faults.

**Noise (MiMo residential)** — SPL at property line per sound permit; bass-heavy program managed with cardioid sub array aim; curfew enforced; community-relations contact on call.

**Comms** — Motorola CP200d: ch1 production, ch2 security, ch3 medical, ch4 stage/deck, ch5 bar/ops. Backup WhatsApp `T3-{{SLUG}}-Showday`. Show-stop is an all-channel call.

**Chain of Command** — Producer → Tigre EP → GHXSTSHIP Dir of Production → Spotify LATAM Casa lead. Halt authority: Safety Director (safety), Producer (operational), Spotify lead (editorial).
