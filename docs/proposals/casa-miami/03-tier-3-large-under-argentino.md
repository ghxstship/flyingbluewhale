## Project Overview

**Campaign Storyline.** Under Argentino is Spotify's platform for the Argentine underground reshaping youth culture — RKT, trap, cumbia 420, the bedroom-to-stadium pipeline. The scene is at a tipping point at home; the gap is that Gen Z doesn't yet read Spotify as the platform that elevates the new. This event closes that gap by bringing El Under into the U.S. for the first time — bold, raw, physical — and making the Casa the epicenter of a movement landing on new soil. Three acts, OOH that reads as cultural statement (porteño visual language, not branded advertising), Argentine F&B, and a community-built room.

## Emergency Procedures — Under Argentino Overlays

Inherits Tier 3 baseline. Under Argentino-specific:

- **International talent contingency.** 3 acts traveling from Buenos Aires — visa/flight risk. Hold a remote-cameo + DJ-extended fallback if an act is delayed; Spotify-supplied "the scene is bigger than any one set" hold messaging. Confirm arrivals T-2; backline is shared so a missing act doesn't break the others' set.
- **Changeover crowd surge.** RKT/cumbia crowds push to the stage at drops; barricade at stage lip, front-of-stage security detail, changeover DJ bridge prevents dead-air crush between the three acts.
- **Colectivo install safety.** The bus statement piece is engineered for load/occupancy (photo moment inside); capacity-limited with an attendant; egress path around it preserved; no climbing — security-monitored.
- **Fernet/Malbec pacing.** Argentine bar runs strong + social; water station + last-call 01:00 + recovery zone; refusal-of-service briefed.
- **Confetti slip hazard.** Headline confetti moment → strike includes immediate floor sweep before guest-out path opens.
