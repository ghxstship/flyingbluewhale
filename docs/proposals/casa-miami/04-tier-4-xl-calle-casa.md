## Project Overview

**Open-Brief Concept.** Calle Casa is a Miami block party folded into the Casa — the literal street energy of Calle Ocho (dominoes, croquetas, fileteado-meets-graffiti murals, a record-store soundsystem) staged as a two-stage, market-anchored cultural takeover. It connects Spotify to Miami's Gen Z Latin community by making Spotify the facilitator: the platform builds the room and hands it to Sweat Records, a Little Havana cultural nonprofit, local vendors, and local artists. The bill is Miami-Latin underground (Nick León, INVT, Ms Nina + local collaborators). Timed to **Miami Art Week** for maximum tastemaker density and "only in Miami" credibility.

**Why it's "Only in Miami."** The partner is a real Miami institution. The food is from Calle Ocho. The art is commissioned from local muralists. The signature moment is a working domino plaza. None of it ports to another city — which is the point.

## Emergency Procedures — Calle Casa Overlays

Inherits Tier 4 baseline. Calle Casa-specific:

- **Art Week density.** Dec 4 is peak Art Week — citywide traffic, parking scarcity, tastemaker walk-up pressure. Valet + shuttle + rideshare zone planned with MPD off-duty traffic detail; hard 400 cap, list-priority with a small tastemaker walk-up buffer managed at the door.
- **Two-stage + patio + market crowd flow.** Staggered set times and DJ bridges prevent main↔patio crush; market + domino plaza distribute dwell so the floor never single-points; patio sub-count tracked separately against weather + noise limits.
- **Vendor market safety.** Each Calle Ocho vendor verified for permits + insurance + fire extinguisher in Advance; open-flame cooking permitted only in patio market zone with FD sign-off; allergen labeling enforced.
- **Live mural / commission.** Paint fumes ventilated (patio/lobby airflow); wet-paint barrier; artist materials stored per fire code.
- **Community-relations on call.** The Discovery-phase MOU includes a Little Havana + MiMo neighborhood contact reachable all night for noise/goodwill; patio music curfew enforced earlier than indoor.
- **Domino plaza.** Working tables = glassware/seating density; attendant-managed; egress around plaza preserved.
