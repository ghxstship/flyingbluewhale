## Standout differentiators per platform

### Procore

1. Broadest end-to-end coverage in one platform — precon, PM, quality/safety, financials, analytics.
2. 300+ App Marketplace, the largest third-party ecosystem in construction.
3. Native financials with AIA-style invoicing, budgets, change mgmt, and accounting connectors — the practical "one system of record" for large commercial GCs.

### Autodesk Construction Cloud (Forma)

1. BIM dominance — native Revit/Navisworks/AutoCAD lineage, real clash detection (Model Coordination), design-to-field traceability.
2. BuildingConnected — the largest network for bid invitations.
3. Document management heritage from PlanGrid + BIM 360 Docs — fastest sheet-set publishing and version control; 2026 added spec-to-file/sheet/RFI/submittal linking.

### Oracle Aconex

1. Neutral, owner-controlled collaboration: every party is a peer, immutable correspondence records — courtroom-defensible.
2. Transmittals and audit trail unmatched.
3. Models module + tight integration with P6 + Unifier under the Oracle Smart Construction Platform.

### Oracle Primavera Unifier

1. PMIS depth for capital programs — cash flow, funding sources, cost workflows.
2. Tight P6 schedule integration for pay-app verification.
3. Highly configurable business processes for owners, government, transit, healthcare.

### Trimble Viewpoint (Vista / Spectrum / ProjectSight)

1. True construction ERP — GL, AP/AR, certified/union/multi-state payroll, AIA billing, equipment cost.
2. Heavy/civil orientation (HCSS-adjacent, Trimble GPS/machine-control synergy).
3. ProjectSight as the field/PM front-end for the same financial backbone.

### Buildertrend

1. Best-in-class residential UX: client portal with selections, change orders, financing, payments.
2. Lead-to-closeout in one tool for the under-$10M segment.
3. Built-in takeoff + integrated QuickBooks/Xero — small-builder friendly, $499/mo entry point.

### Fieldwire by Hilti

1. Field-first task management with pin-on-plan, offline-first sync.
2. Punch list workflow — categories, templates, locations, automatic PDF reports — beloved by superintendents.
3. Used on 2M+ projects globally; Hilti tool-tracking adjacency.

### Bluebeam Revu + Cloud

1. Markup gold standard — the de-facto PDF tool for architects, engineers, estimators.
2. Quantity takeoff with calibrated measurement, custom tool sets, real-time cost columns — exports clean QTO data.
3. Studio Sessions for live multi-party markup; Cloud Field Tools extend punch/RFI to iOS.

### Raken

1. Daily report in <5 minutes — weather, manpower, equipment, photos, sign-off — the speed king.
2. 500+ OSHA-compliant toolbox talks with digital sign-off.
3. Lightweight, sub-friendly, plays nicely under Procore/ACC for GC roll-up.

### e-Builder + Kahua

1. Owner/public-sector orientation — capital planning, sources of funds, contract administration, asset handover.
2. Kahua's low-code Kahuas (apps) for custom workflows; Noa embedded AI in 2026.
3. Asset-centric handover ties construction outputs to FM systems.

### Sage / Foundation

1. Construction accounting depth — certified payroll, union, prevailing wage, lien waiver management.
2. WIP reporting and job cost detail at GAAP-audit grade.
3. Multi-entity / multi-state / bonding-friendly reports out of the box.

### OpenSpace / DroneDeploy / Matterport

1. Hands-free 360° capture (helmet-mount) auto-mapped to floor plans via Spatial AI (OpenSpace).
2. Drone photogrammetry with RTK/PPK for survey-grade earthworks (DroneDeploy Aerial).
3. Time-slider progress comparison, AI punch detection, integration into Procore/ACC issue workflows.

## 2026 table-stakes checklist (55 items)

These are the minimum-credible features any serious construction PM platform must answer "yes" to in 2026. Grouped into 8 categories.

### A. Identity, Access & Cross-Org (5)

1. SSO via SAML/OIDC (Okta, Entra ID, Google).
2. SCIM provisioning for joiner/mover/leaver.
3. Multi-tenant role/permission matrix at company × project × tool granularity.
4. External-party invite (sub, owner, AE) without paying per-seat for read access.
5. Audit log of every state change, exportable for litigation/discovery.

### B. Documents & Drawings (8)

6. Sheet-set publishing with auto-versioning, slip-sheet/overlay diff, hyperlink callouts.
7. PDF markup with calibrated measurement, custom tool sets, scaled symbols.
8. BIM model viewer for IFC, RVT, NWD/NWC, with section/measure/markup in browser.
9. Clash detection or model-coordination integration (native or via partner).
10. Specifications module with section linking to RFIs, submittals, and sheets.
11. Transmittals with read receipts and immutable timestamps.
12. Folder-level permissions plus document-level holds for legal/contractual control.
13. Offline drawing access on iPad/Android tablets with conflict-free sync.

### C. Communications & Workflow (6)

14. RFI workflow with configurable types, ball-in-court, due dates, escalation.
15. Submittal workflow with review cycles, spec-section linking, distribution rules.
16. Punch list with pin-on-plan, photo, assignee, location, status, PDF report.
17. Daily report capturing weather (auto), manpower, equipment, work performed, notes, photos, signatures.
18. Meeting minutes with action items that auto-create tasks.
19. Email-in: forward to a project address to file correspondence into the record.

### D. Schedule, Budget, Contracts (8)

20. Native Gantt with critical path, plus P6 / MS Project / Asta import.
21. 3-week / 6-week lookahead view for supers.
22. Budget with CSI MasterFormat (and CSC for Canada) and AIA G703 schedule of values.
23. Original budget / approved changes / pending changes / projected costs columns.
24. Contracts module with prime + sub contracts, retention, NTE, allowance tracking.
25. PCO → CO workflow with cost impact, schedule impact, ball-in-court.
26. Change order routing with markup rules (% on labor, equipment, sub, etc.).
27. Forecast / EAC at project and portfolio level.

### E. Field Execution (8)

28. Mobile-first iOS + Android apps with parity to web for core workflows.
29. Offline-first sync with conflict resolution.
30. Crew clock-in with geofence enforcement + photo capture; export to payroll.
31. Equipment time + utilization tracking.
32. Inspections / QA-QC checklists with templates, signatures, fail→issue conversion.
33. Safety: JHA/PTP creation, 500+ toolbox talks with digital sign-off, near-miss + incident, OSHA 300/300A/301 auto-generation.
34. 360° / photo / video capture integration (OpenSpace, DroneDeploy, Matterport).
35. Push notifications + per-kind opt-out for assignments, mentions, approvals.

### F. Financials (10)

36. AIA G702/G703 pay app generation with retainage, stored materials, prior periods.
37. Subcontractor pay app collection with lien-waiver capture (conditional/unconditional, partial/final).
38. AP invoice OCR with PO/sub-contract matching.
39. AR with progress billing, T&M, fixed price, retention release.
40. Job cost ledger with committed/incurred/forecast/EAC columns.
41. Time-card → certified payroll / union / multi-state / prevailing wage.
42. WIP report (over/under billing) suitable for surety/bonding.
43. Native accounting or certified two-way connector to Sage 100/300, Foundation, QuickBooks, Viewpoint, Acumatica.
44. Multi-currency + multi-entity consolidation (for international GCs).
45. Bank-grade payment rails (ACH, Connect, virtual cards) for sub/vendor payouts.

### G. Closeout & Handover (4)

46. Punch list close-out workflow with sign-offs by trade.
47. Warranty period tracking with reminder cadence to subs.
48. O&M manual / asset handover package generation with linked spec sections, submittals, and as-builts.
49. Final lien waiver collection at job close.

### H. Analytics, AI & Ecosystem (6)

50. Portfolio dashboards with drill-down to project, with role-based views.
51. Resource planning across projects (workforce, equipment, fleet).
52. AI assistant for natural-language search over project documents and metadata (table stakes in 2026 after Procore Copilot, ACC, Kahua Noa, Newforma Vojo, Bridgit AI all shipped).
53. Predictive risk scoring (schedule slip, cost overrun, safety, sub default).
54. Open REST/GraphQL API + webhooks + sandbox environment for partner integrations.
55. App marketplace / partner ecosystem with at least 100 certified integrations covering accounting, ERP, scheduling, BIM, reality capture, e-sign, prequal/COI, payments, time-tracking, fleet/telematics.
